How to Export Your TextBox Contacts

  1. Log into your TextBox account
  2. Go to Contacts
  3. Click Export
  4. Download the file (.xlsx or .csv)
  5. Upload that file below as your “Current TextBox Export”

This file is used to preserve existing contact details that are not present in your new source file.
